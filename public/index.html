FAQ

Questions, answered.

What do I actually get for £49 a month?
A photoreal 3D configurator, embedded on your website under your branding, that lets your buyers design and price a veranda, carport, garden room or pergola. Every time a buyer asks for the formal quote, the lead arrives as an email in your inbox in real time — with their name, phone, the exact spec, and the price. Unlimited designs, unlimited emails. That's the whole product.
How is your 3D different from competitor configurators?
Most veranda configurators look like cardboard cutouts — flat colours, fake shadows, the same generic SketchUp model with your logo on it. Ours is genuinely photoreal: studio-grade lighting, real material physics, smooth on every device. The buyer doesn't see a 3D widget. They see their veranda. That's what closes the sale.
Which products does the configurator cover?
Verandas, carports, garden rooms and pergolas — all four product categories run on the same engine. You send us your range and we model each variation under your branding, with your finishes, dimensions and pricing built in.
How does the lead actually arrive?
As a normal email, in your inbox, in real time. The buyer fills in their name, email and phone to receive the PDF quote. The same moment they hit "send", you get a structured copy of the lead — contact details, the full spec they designed, the price they accepted, and the PDF attached. No dashboard, no CRM, no waiting.
Can I put it on my own website with my own branding?
Absolutely. The configurator runs under your name, logo, colours and domain. Buyers think they're using your software — because it is yours to use. We never appear in front of your customer.
Does it really work on mobile without dropping quality?
Yes. The renderer is engineered to hit 60fps on a four-year-old phone without an app install. Buyers get the same photoreal experience on the train, on the sofa or stood in the back garden imagining where the veranda will go.
How fast can I be live and getting lead emails?
Seven days. Once you send your products, options and pricing, we set everything up under your brand and hand it back ready to drop on your website. Most veranda sellers are getting their first lead emails within a week.
